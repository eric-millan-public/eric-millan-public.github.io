Design Tokens

Understanding and using design tokens effectively in projects.

What are Design Tokens?

Design tokens are the visual design atoms of the design system â€” specifically, they are named entities that store visual design attributes.

Examples of Design Tokens

Here are some common examples of design tokens:

  • Color: Base, brand, and accent colors.
  • Font Sizes: Typography scale - e.g., normal, large, extra-large.
  • Spacing: Margins and paddings used throughout the design.
